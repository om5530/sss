Our story

Every good day starts at 5 a.m.

That’s when our ovens come on. Butter gets folded, dough gets shaped and the first trays go in — so everything you order today was baked today.

Small batch, on purpose

Small batches, zero shortcuts

We bake in small batches because butter, chocolate and patience all behave better that way. Whatever comes out of the ovens each morning is what we sell — and when a bake runs out, it’s gone until tomorrow’s trays.

No premixes, no week-old freezer stock. Croissants are laminated by hand, brownies leave the oven while their centres are still molten, and the pizza oven burns real wood from open to close.
